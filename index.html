Pig Dice Game


Game Rules

The game has 2 players, playing in rounds.In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he wishes. Each result gets added to his total score but if the player rolls a 1, all his score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn.The player can choose to Hold, which means that his score gets added to his total score. After that, it's the next player's turn.The first player to get 100 points on the total score wins the game!
